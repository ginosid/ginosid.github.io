A few steps to get your started...

Figuring out what an ardunio is may be a good place to start?

This website proved usedful for me (the non-programmer in the relationship) Ardunio Guide and Introduction

Buy the Ardunio off ebay and eagerly wait for it to arrive.

"I can't wait for my arduino to arrive" Petar Kolaric
"You mean our ardunio?" Genevieve Helson

Once the package arrives...

  1. Jump up and down for at least 30 seconds
  2. Open said package
  3. Take note of what the hell everything is
  4. Check out Makers Spaces for a simple guide to get you going

TIP: You may need to purchase some batteries online separately for the remote control: Lithium Button Cell Coin Batteries 3V. I used Amazon to help me with my purchase:

Amazon Lithium Batteries Pack of 5
